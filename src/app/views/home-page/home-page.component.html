Stations of San Andreas

Los Santos

At times you may need to put some distance between yourself and those that are in pursuit. If this is the case, we recommend that you make your way to LSX or take the first train out of Los Santos. Train stations are located within the city limits at Vinewood and in Little Mexico.

San Fierro

Need to get out of San Fierro in a quick fashion? Don’t have money for a plane ticket? Or maybe you just don’t like to fly? Whatever the reason may be…you can still hop on a train, Cranberry Station is located in the heart of the Doherty district so make haste.

Las Venturas

You got no cash, maxed out your credit cards, pawned your Crowex President and still owe the house 50 large. You have to get the hell out of Las Venturas! Make your way to the Prickle Pine district and catch the Brown Streak out of town.
